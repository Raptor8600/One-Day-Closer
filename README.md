# One Day Closer 🛋️✨

A private, gentle, and supportive journal for anyone navigating benzodiazepine recovery.

## ✨ Features
- **Daily Journal**: Log how you feel and track your symptoms with a simple "Good, Mixed, Hard" rating.
- **Supportive Insights**: Get daily affirmations and reassuring facts from medical literature (like the Ashton Manual) right at the top of your screen.
- **Symptom Patterns**: View charts of your progress to see the "waves and windows" of your healing.
- **Box Breathing**: A specialized 4-4-4-4 breathing tool to help calm your nervous system.
- **Privacy-First**: Your data stays 100% on your device. No cloud, no accounts, no tracking.

## 📱 How to install on your iPhone (Recommended)
This app is designed to look and feel like a professional app on your iPhone.

1.  Open the website in **Safari** on your iPhone. https://raptor8600.github.io/One-Day-Closer/
2.  Tap the **Share** button (the square with the up arrow at the bottom).
3.  Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4.  Confirm the name and tap **Add**.
5.  Launch the app from your home screen for a full, premium experience!
